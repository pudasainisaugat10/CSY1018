Site Report

About Assignment

This is the site report for my assignment of WEB-1(module number CSY1018). This report shows the entire details of assignment, about my learning techniques, the ups and down while performing the task and shows about the problems and solutions while doing the task. Its shows about my design, my information and my skills.

The assignment was given on 2021, Tuesday May 11, 9:05 PM through via mail. After the mail came I was unknown about the assignment and after that our course lecturer Mr. Ankit Thapa sends us an email on Sunday May 16, 8:25 PM for discussion on assignment. Similarly on Wednesday May 19, we got an email from our lecturer for another discussion class. After that I started to research on given topic. The purpose of the assignment is to asses our ability to create a web page using HTML and CSS and managing our code using GitHub. We was tasked to develop a website with at least five pages that are Homepage, Portfolio, Contact, Curriculum Vitae and Report. In addition to the website we need to make a short video brief about our web design. After the completion of give task we had to submit our assignment through NILE (NORTHAMPTON INTEGRATED LEARNING ENVIRONMENT).
